About the Team

NaVi (Natus Vincere) is one of the most renowned esports teams in the world. Founded in 2009 in Ukraine, they compete in multiple disciplines such as CS:GO, Dota 2, Rainbow Six, and more. In 2021, NaVi became CS:GO world champions by winning the PGL Major.
